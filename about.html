Welcome to THE SMART SCHOOL

Established in Karachi in 2016, The Smart School Haqqani is today one of the best private schools in the city offering 21st Century skills along with education. We are a family of 60+ faculty members and 700+ students. We are continuously striving hard to offer the best, effective and fun studying environment for students and have been successful in doing so.

700+ Students
60+ Faculty Members

Our Motto

“To empower the future of the nation through an affordable yet standardized quality of education”

Our Mission

To establish a network that imparts high quality education without discrimination. We aim to develop youth who are holistically groomed and empowered to meet the challenging world head-on. Employing the latest teaching techniques, our mission is to inculcate values, morals and ethics amongst our students as well as grooming them for a successful future.

Message from the Headmistress

At The Smart School, our motto is to inculcate quality education for all regardless of their religion, race or ability to afford education. For us, education is a pursuit that goes far beyond a qualification and we aim to ensure that as educators, we strengthen our economy by making it accessible to everyone enabling Pakistan to become a nation of critical thinkers, problem solvers and enablers who are ready to face challenges with courage and conviction.
