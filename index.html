Our Profile

Established in Karachi in 1978, The City Schoolis today one of the largest private school networks across the globe. Having more than 500 schools with over 150,000 students, we continue to expand our reach in other countries including United Arab Emirates, Malaysia, Philippines, Oman, and Saudi Arabia, to meet the demands for quality education as well as to ensure a strong global presence.
